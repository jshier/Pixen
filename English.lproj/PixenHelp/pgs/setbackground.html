Setting a document's backgrounds

So the time has come. You're ready to move on from the neutral gray and white slashed background Pixen ships with. The backgrounds configurator will help you with this.

To set a document's background:

Choose a template from the left side of the backgrounds configurator on which you'd like to base your document's background.

Drag the template into one of the areas on the right, for either the main or the alternate background.

A copy of this template and saved with the document; now you can change its name, colors, and other applicable parameters.
