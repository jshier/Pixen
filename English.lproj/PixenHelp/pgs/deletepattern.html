Deleting a saved pattern

If you've saved or imported a pattern and decide that you don't want it anymore, it's easy to get rid of it.

Open the saved patterns drawer if it is not already open by clicking on the pattern editor's Saved Patterns toolbar button.

Select the pattern in the drawer that you want to discard.

Press the delete key or the delete button at the bottom of the drawer.
