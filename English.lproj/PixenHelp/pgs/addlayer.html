Adding a new layer

In order to actually make use of most of Pixen's layer features, you'll have to be working with more than one layer.

To add a layer:

Click the plus button in the layers drawer.

or

Use the Layers > New Layer (⇧⌘N)

If the layers drawer isn't already open when you've added a new layer, it will be opened for you. Additionally, the name field of the new layer will be focused for you, ready to enter the desired name. Just press enter when you're done.
