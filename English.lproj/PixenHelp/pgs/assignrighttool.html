Assigning a tool to the right mouse button

For those of you with multi-button mice, you might find it extremely useful to specify a tool and color to be executed by your right mouse button in complement to your left. Pixen makes this easy.

To assign a tool to your right mouse button:

Look at the tool palette and find the right tool disclosure area ().

If the arrow is pointing to the right, click it, and the right tool configurator will open.

The right tool area behaves just like the left tool area: just click a tool to select it.

If you don't have a two-button mouse, you can still leverage this feature: holding the control key emulates the right mouse button.
